What are wigner plots? (or wigner functions)

A way to visualise a quantum state in something that looks like a classical phase space
space of position x and momentum p

QM - cant know both x and p simultaneously, can describe the probability-like distribution using Wigner function

Coherent state: round gaussian blob centred at (x0, p0)
Squeezed state: elliptical blob, squeezed in one direction stretched in the other
Cat state: two blobs with interference fringes (alternating positive\negative regions)

Can be negative - makes it quantum

NN takes wigner image and recognises something about underlying quantum state
    - can make it classify the type of state
